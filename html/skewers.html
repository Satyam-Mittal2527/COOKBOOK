Chicken Skewers!

Ingredients:

  • 500 grams chicken breast, cut into cubes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  • Wooden or metal skewers

Instructions:

1
In a large bowl, combine olive oil, minced garlic, paprika, ground cumin, dried oregano, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
2
Add the cubed chicken to the bowl and toss to coat evenly. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
3
Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
4
Thread the marinated chicken cubes onto the skewers.
5
Grill the chicken skewers for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is fully cooked and has grill marks.
6
Remove the skewers from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes.
7
Garnish with fresh parsley and serve with lemon wedges on the side.
