Juju Dhau Recipe!

Ingredients

    For Juju Dhau:

  • 1 litre whole milk
  • 1/2 cup yogurt (as a starter)
  • 1/4 cup sugar (optional, for sweetness)
  • 1/2 tsp ground cardamom (optional, for flavor)
  • A few strands of saffron (optional, for flavor and color)
  • Clay or earthenware pots (for traditional setting)

Instructions

    ⤷Prepare the Milk:

  1. In a large pot, bring the whole milk to a boil.
  2. Reduce the heat and let the milk simmer for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally to prevent it from sticking to the bottom of the pot.
  3. Add sugar, ground cardamom, and saffron (if using) to the milk. Mix well and continue to simmer for another 5 minutes.
  4. Remove the pot from the heat and let the milk cool to a lukewarm temperature (about 110°F or 43°C).

    ⤷Prepare the Yogurt Mixture:

  1. In a small bowl, mix the yogurt starter with a little lukewarm milk to make a smooth mixture.
  2. Add the yogurt mixture to the lukewarm milk and mix well.

    ⤷Set the Yogurt:

  1. Pour the mixture into clay or earthenware pots for a traditional setting. If you don't have clay pots, you can use any clean container.
  2. Cover the pots with a cloth or lid and place them in a warm, undisturbed area for about 6-8 hours or until the yogurt is set and firm.
