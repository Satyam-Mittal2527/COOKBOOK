Grilled Sandwich Delight!

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, or your favorite blend)
  • 1/2 cup cooked and shredded chicken (optional)
  • 1 small tomato, sliced
  • 1/4 cup bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: fresh basil or cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

1
Prepare the Ingredients: Slice the tomato, bell pepper, and onion. Shred the cheese and cooked chicken (if using).
2
Butter the Bread: Spread a thin layer of butter on one side of each slice of bread.
3
Assemble the Sandwich: Place the buttered side down on a hot skillet or griddle. Layer cheese, chicken, tomato slices, bell pepper, and onion on top. Season with salt and pepper.
4
Top with another slice of bread, buttered side up.
5
Grill the Sandwich: Cook on medium heat until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted, about 3-4 minutes per side.
6
Garnish and Serve: Remove from the skillet, slice in half, garnish with fresh basil or cilantro, and serve hot.
