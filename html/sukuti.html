Sukuti Recipe!

Ingredients

    For Sukuti:

  • 500g dried meat (buffalo, lamb, or beef)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp mustard oil
  • 1 tbsp roasted sesame seeds
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

    ⤷Prepare the Sukuti:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the dried meat with turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, chili powder, and salt. Mix well to coat the meat evenly with the spices.
  2. In a pan, heat mustard oil and add minced ginger and garlic. Sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add the spiced meat to the pan and cook until it is browned and cooked through.
  4. Remove the meat from the heat and let it cool slightly.
  5. In a bowl, mix the cooked meat with chopped onions, green chilies, roasted sesame seeds, lemon juice, and fresh coriander leaves.
