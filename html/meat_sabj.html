Meat Sabji!

Ingredients:

  • 500 grams meat (chicken, mutton, or your choice), cubed
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, minced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 potato, diced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

1
Heat vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat.
2
Add the finely chopped onion and saut√© until golden brown.
3
Stir in the minced garlic and ginger, and cook for another minute until fragrant.
4
Add the cubed meat to the pot and cook until it starts to brown.
5
Add the turmeric powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, garam masala, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well to coat the meat with the spices.
6
Add the diced tomatoes, potato, carrot, and green bell pepper. Stir to combine.
7
Cover and cook on low heat for about 30-35 minutes, or until the meat and vegetables are tender.
8
Adjust seasoning with salt if needed.
9
Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with rice or roti.
