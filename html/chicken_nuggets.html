Chicken Nuggets Recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (cut into bite-sized pieces)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs (beaten)
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs (plain or seasoned)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Oil for frying (vegetable, canola, or your choice)
  • Your favorite dipping sauces

Instructions:

1
Prepare the Breading Stations: Set up three bowls. In the first bowl, place the flour. In the second bowl, beat the eggs. In the third bowl, combine the breadcrumbs, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder.
2
Coat the Chicken: Dredge each chicken piece in the flour, shaking off any excess. Dip it in the beaten eggs, then coat it with the breadcrumb mixture. Make sure each piece is well-coated.
3
Heat the Oil: In a large skillet or deep fryer, heat oil over medium-high heat. You need enough oil to submerge the chicken pieces.
4
Fry the Nuggets: Carefully place the breaded chicken pieces into the hot oil. Fry in batches to avoid overcrowding the pan. Cook each batch for about 3-4 minutes per side, or until the nuggets are golden brown and cooked through.
5
Drain and Serve: Use a slotted spoon to remove the chicken nuggets from the oil. Place them on a plate lined with paper towels to drain any excess oil. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauces.
