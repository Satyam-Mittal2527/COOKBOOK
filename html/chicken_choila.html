Chicken Choila Recipe!

Ingredients

    For Chicken Choila:

  • 500g boneless chicken, diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp mustard oil
  • 1 tbsp roasted sesame seeds
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

    ⤷Prepare the Chicken Choila:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the diced chicken, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, chili powder, and salt. Mix well to coat the chicken evenly with the spices.
  2. In a pan, heat mustard oil and add minced ginger and garlic. Sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add the marinated chicken to the pan and cook until the chicken is browned and cooked through.
  4. Remove the chicken from the heat and let it cool slightly.
  5. In a bowl, mix the cooked chicken with chopped onions, green chilies, roasted sesame seeds, lemon juice, and fresh coriander leaves.
