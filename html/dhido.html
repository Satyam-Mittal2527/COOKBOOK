Dhido Recipe!

Ingredients

    For Dhido:

  • 2 cups buckwheat flour or millet flour
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp ghee or clarified butter (optional, for flavor)

Instructions

    â¤·Prepare the Dhido:

  1. In a large pot, bring 4 cups of water to a boil.
  2. Add salt to the boiling water.
  3. Gradually add the buckwheat or millet flour to the boiling water, stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  4. Reduce the heat to low and continue stirring the mixture with a wooden spatula.
  5. Cook the Dhido for about 10-15 minutes, stirring constantly, until it thickens and pulls away from the sides of the pot.
  6. If using ghee or clarified butter, add it to the Dhido and mix well.
