Aloo Tama Recipe!

Ingredients

    For Aloo Tama:

  • 4-5 medium potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup bamboo shoots (fresh or canned), sliced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • 1/4 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

    ⤷Prepare the Aloo Tama:

  1. In a large pot, heat oil and add mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds, and asafoetida. Let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions, garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Sauté until the onions turn golden brown.
  3. Add diced potatoes, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, chili powder, and salt. Cook for a few minutes until the potatoes start to soften.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they are soft and mushy.
  5. Add the sliced bamboo shoots and mix well.
  6. Pour in enough water to cover the mixture. Bring it to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer until the potatoes are fully cooked and the flavors are well combined.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
